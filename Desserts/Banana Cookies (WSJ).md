# Banana Cookies (WSJ)

- Servings 16 — 18
- 1 cup mashed bananas (about 3 Firm bananas)
- 2 cups almond meal/flour (may substitute finely ground skinless almonds)
- 1/3 cup maple syrup
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/4 cup demerara sugar (may substitute dark brown sugar; see headnote)

Preheat the oven to 325 degrees. Line a baking sheet with a silicone liner or parchment paper.
Combine the mashed bananas, almond meal/flour, maple syrup and 1/4 teaspoon of the ground cinnamon in a mixing bowl, stirring to incorporate. Whisk together the sugar and the remaining 1 1/4 teaspoons cinnamon in a shallow bowl.

Roll 16 to 18 spoonfuls of the banana mixture into balls, Press them into the sugar-cinnamon mixture to coat all over, spacing the cookies an inch apart on the baking sheet as you work, then slightly flatten each one on the sheet. Bake (middle rack) for 35 to 40 minutes or until golden and just crisp to the touch, turning the baking sheet front to back halfway through.
Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet before serving or storing.
