Chaeschueli  
  
For the shortcrust pastry dough:  
  
160 gms all purpose flour (1 cup+), refrigerated   
0.5 tsp salt  
0.5 tsp salt  
100 gms cold unsalted butter, diced small  
3 tbsp ice cold water  
3 tbsp ice cold water  
  
Add the salt to the flour and mix gently.   
Tip the butter in and coat with flour.   
Begin to mix the butter into the flour by pressing the pieces until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.   
Do not knead.   
Now add the water, 1 tbsp at a time till everything comes together (you may or may not need all of the water). If the dough looks wet add a little flour and roll into a thick cylinder.   
Cool for at least an hour in the fridge.   
Take out and as soon as the dough is malleable, cut the cylinder into 8 discs.   
Leave 7 in the fridge and roll the 8th about 6 to 7 inches across. Lay into a pastry pan and prick the base with a fork and return to fridge.   
Repeat for remaining disks.   
  
Blind baking:  
Set oven to 375 degrees F.   
  
Line each pastry case with parchment paper and add some raw rice to weigh it down.  Return to fridge while the other cases are prepared.   
Bake for 15 minutes.   
Remove from oven and allow to cool.   
Carefully remove the parchment with the rice and set aside. The rice can be saved for other blind bakings.   
  
Filling:  
  
150 gms Gruyère cheese, grated  
2 eggs  
100 ml milk  
100 ml milk  
[150 ml](x-apple-data-detectors://embedded-result/1267) full cream  
0.25 tsp nutmeg grated  
0.25 tsp nutmeg grated  
Salt and pepper  
Fresh thyme leaves  
  
Whisk together the eggs, cream, milk, nutmeg, salt, pepper and thyme leaves.   
Divide the grated cheese among the tart shells. Then divide the egg mixture over them.   
Bake at 375 for about 15 minutes.   
Enjoy!
