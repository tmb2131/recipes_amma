Cheese Biscuits (G)  
40 - 50  
Ingredients: 200g plain flour; 1 tsp salt; ¼ tsp cayenne pepper; 200g grated strong cheddar or blue cheese; 200g cold diced butter; 1 egg, beaten.  
  
Method: Put the flour, salt and cayenne in the food processor bowl, mix quickly, then add the cheese and butter. Run the motor until the mixture forms a smooth ball, taking care not to overwork it. Wrap in clingfilm and freeze until needed. To cook, preheat the oven to 190C/375F/gas mark 5. Use the pastry cold, a quarter at a time. Beat with a rolling pin a few times, then, on a lightly floured board, roll to 2mm thick. Cut into circles with a 5mm or 6mm cutter, or into squares. Form the trimmings into another ball and roll out as before. Put on a baking tray (no greasing necessary), chill for five minutes, then eggwash – this helps hold the sablés together (try to avoid the eggwash dripping on to the tray). Bake for 12 minutes, until golden brown.
