Coffee Martini  
  
The cocktail is a fairly simple concoction.   
++[Liquor.com](https://www.liquor.com/recipes/espresso-martini-2/)++   
  
  
2 ounces vodka, 1/2 ounce coffee liqueur (such as Kahlua), 1 ounce of espresso (freshly brewed or cold brew concentrate)   
1/2 ounce simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice,   
shake until well-chilled.   
Then strain it into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a few coffee beans.
