# Coffee Martini

The cocktail is a fairly simple concoction.

<br />

2 ounces vodka, 1/2 ounce coffee liqueur (such as Kahlua), 1 ounce of espresso (freshly brewed or cold brew concentrate), 1/2 ounce simple syrup to a shaker filled with ice, shake until well-chilled.

<br />

Then strain it into a chilled cocktail glass and garnish with a few coffee beans.
