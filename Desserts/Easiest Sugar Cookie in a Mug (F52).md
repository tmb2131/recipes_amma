Easiest Sugar Cookie in a Mug (F52)  
  
  
* 		1 tablespoon unsalted butter  
* 		1 tablespoon granulated sugar  
* 		1 tablespoon light brown sugar  
* 		1/4 teaspoon pure vanilla extract  
* 		1/4 teaspoon almond extract  
* 		1/8 teaspoon kosher salt  
* 		1 large egg yolk  
* 		2 tablespoons all-purpose flour  
* 		1 tablespoon powdered sugar  
* 		Sprinkles, for garnishing  
  
  
  
* 		Spray a small microwave-safe mug or a 5-ounce ramekin with vegetable oil spray.  Place butter in the mug & melt in microwave, about 30 seconds.  Add two kinds of sugar, vanilla extract, almond extract, and salt. Stir to combine.  Add the egg yolk and mix thoroughly.  Add flour and powdered sugar and stir until combined.  Top with sprinkles and cook in the microwave for about 55-60 seconds.  Let cool for 3-5 minutes before serving.
