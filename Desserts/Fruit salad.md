Fruit salad  
  
Mixed cut fruit  
  
  
⅔ cup fresh orange juice ⅓ cup fresh lemon juice ⅓ cup packed brown sugar ½ teaspoon grated orange zest   
½ teaspoon grated lemon zest   
1 teaspoon grated ginger  
  
1 teaspoon vanilla extract  
  
Bring orange juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, grated ginger, orange zest, and lemon zest to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer until slightly thickened, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat, and stir in vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.
