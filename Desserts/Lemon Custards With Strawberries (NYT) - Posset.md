# Lemon Custards With Strawberries (NYT) - Posset

- 2 **cups heavy cream
- ⅔ **cup granulated sugar
- 2 **teaspoons finely grated lemon zest (from 1 to 2 lemons)
- Pinch of fine sea salt
- ⅓ **cup fresh lemon juice (from 2 to 3 lemons)
- FOR THE STRAWBERRY TOPPING:
- 1 **cup sliced strawberries
- 1 to 2 **teaspoons granulated sugar
- Freshly ground black pepper, for serving

1. In a medium saucepan, combine cream, sugar, lemon zest and salt over medium-high heat. Bring to simmer, stirring frequently to dissolve sugar. Simmer vigorously until mixture thickens slightly, about 4 to 5 minutes.
2. <br />
3. Remove from heat and stir in lemon juice. Let sit until mixture has cooled slightly and a skin forms on top, about 20 minutes.
4. <br />
5. Stir mixture, then strain through fine-mesh strainer into a measuring cup with a spout; discard zest. Pour mixture evenly into six 6-ounce ramekins or small bowls.
6. <br />
7. Refrigerate, uncovered, until set, at least 3 hours.
8. <br />
9. As the custards chill, prepare the strawberry topping: Toss strawberries and sugar in a small mixing bowl. Let fruit macerate at room temperature for 30 minutes to 1 hour, until the sugar is dissolved.
10. <br />
11. To serve, top each lemon custard with some strawberry topping and grind black pepper on top.
