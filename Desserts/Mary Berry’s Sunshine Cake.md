Mary Berry’s Sunshine Cake  

Self rising flour 225 gms  
Baking powder 1 tsp  
Sugar 175 gms  

Separate bowl  
Eggs 4  
Oil 225 ml  

Grated carrot 100gms  
Grated zucchini 100gms  
Ripe banana 1  

Whisk egg well with fork.  Add oil plus veggies & fruit.  

Whisk at slow speed until mixed. Do not overmix.  
 Divide into 2 grated&  lined 8” cake pans.  

160 degrees for 25 minutes.  

Icing  
Butter 100 gms  
Cream cheese 100 grams  
Icing sugar 225 gms  
Vanilla extract 1 tsp  

I/2 icing in between cakes. More on top.  

Dried sweet banana chips for topping.
