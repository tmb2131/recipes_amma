Milkshake ice pops (BBC Food)  
Makes 4  
  
## Ingredients  
* 405ml can light condensed milk  
* 1 tsp vanilla bean paste  
* 1 ripe chopped banana  
* 10 strawberries or 3 tbsp chocolate hazelnut spread  
## Method  
* STEP 1Pour the light condensed milk into a food processor and add the vanilla bean paste and chopped banana. Whizz until smooth. Add either the strawberries or chocolate hazelnut spread and whizz again.   
* STEP 2Divide the mixture between 4 paper cups, cover with foil, then push a lolly stick through the foil lid of each cup until you hit the base. Freeze for 4 hrs or until solid. Will keep in the freezer for 2 months.
