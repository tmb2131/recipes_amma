## Orange, Almond and brown butter cake(G)  
Prep **25 min**  
Prep **25 min**  
Cook **35 min**  
Serves **8**  
**120g unsalted butter 125g caster sugar Zest of 2 large oranges  2 large eggs 80ml milk 2 tbsp neutral oil **(vegetable or sunflower)** 100g plain flour 75g almond flour 1 tsp baking powder ¼ tsp bicarbonate of soda ¼ tsp salt**  
  
**Zest strips of 1 lemon & juice**  
**Juice from oranges**  
**60 gms caster sugar**  
  
Grease and line a 20cm round cake tin. Heat the oven to 180C (160C fan)/350F/gas 4.  
Melt the butter in a small saucepan over a medium heat, then let it cook, stirring occasionally, for three to five minutes, until it starts to smell nutty and the milk solids have turned brown. Remove from the heat and pour into a bowl to cool.  
Put the sugar and orange zest in a large bowl, then rub the zest into the sugar with your fingertips, until it resembles wet sand. In a separate bowl or jug, whisk the eggs, milk and oil, pour into the sugar bowl and mix to combine.  
Into a separate bowl, sift the flour, cornmeal, baking powder, bicarb and salt. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry, and mix until smooth. Stir in the cooled brown butter, then pour the batter into the prepared tin.  Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.   
While the cake bakes, prepare syrup.  
* The Syrup  
* use all the lemon juice and enough orange juice to make up 120ml  
* add 80g of sugar  
* just before the cake is ready, bring the syrup straight to the boil  
* drizzle over the cake as soon as it is out the oven  
* allow the cake to cool.  
  
Decorate with zest strips   
  
Leave the cake to cool completely before dusting with a little icing sugar and serving.
