# Pie Crust - All Buttah Pie Dough

- | 1 1/4 | cups all — purpose flour, sifted |
- | | Pinch salt |
- | 8 | tablespoons butter, cut into 1/2 — inch cubes |
- | 1/4 | cup ice water, or more as needed |

1. &#x9;In a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt to combine.
2. <br />
3. Add the butter, tossing the cubes in the flour to coat. Rub the butter into the flour until it is the size of walnut halves (for a flaky crust) or peas (for a mealy crust).
4. <br />
5. Make a well in the center, and add the water a few tablespoons at a time and mix just until the dough comes together.
6. <br />
7. Wrap the dough in plastic and chill well before rolling, forming, and baking.
