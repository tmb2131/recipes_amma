Serves 4

5 Spoon into the basin and smooth the mixture down. Cover the top with clingfilm (or cover with a plate) and microwave on full power for about 2 minutes. Carefully remove the bowl and serve hot with more syrup poured over the top, with a squeeze of lemon juice if you like. Sloshing double cream or custard over it will make it even more delicious.
