  
**Strawberries and Cream With Rose Water and Pistachios (WP)**  
  
1 pound fresh strawberries, hulled and quartered 1/2 cup granulated sugar, divided 1 teaspoon rose water Pinch fine salt 2 cups chilled heavy cream 1 cup raw, unsalted pistachios, toasted and coarsely chopped (see Notes)    In a medium bowl, combine the strawberries, 1/4 cup of the sugar, the rose water and salt, and toss gently to combine. Let sit while you whip the cream.  
 In the bowl of a stand mixer or using a large bowl and a hand mixer, combine the cream with the remaining 1/4 cup of sugar. Starting the mixer on low speed and gradually increasing to medium-high speed, whip the mixture until soft peaks form.  
 Pour the juices from the macerated berries over the whipped cream, and beat on medium speed, just until incorporated.  
 To serve, layer the cream with the berries and pistachios in each bowl.
