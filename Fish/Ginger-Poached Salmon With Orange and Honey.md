**Ginger-Poached Salmon With Orange and Honey**  
(WP)  
  
*Servings: 4*  
Finely grated zest and juice of 1 large orange (about 1/4 cup juice) 1 1/2 cups low-sodium broth 2 teaspoons grated fresh ginger 2 teaspoons finely chopped pickled ginger 2 tablespoons honey 4 center-cut salmon fillets, preferably skinless (about 1 pound total), pin bones removed 1 to 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, to taste  
Directions  
  
  
*Total: 30 mins*  
 In a large (12-inch) skillet over medium-high heat, combine the orange zest and juice, broth, both types of ginger and honey, and bring just to a boil, stirring occasionally. Reduce the heat to medium-low and cook, stirring constantly, for 1 to 2 minutes. Add the salmon, cover and cook for 4 to 5 minutes, then carefully turn the fish over with spatulas or forks, and simmer until cooked through, another 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer the salmon to a plate and cover loosely to keep warm.  
 Increase the heat to medium-high and cook the sauce just until it comes to a boil. Whisk in the butter, stirring until the sauce slightly thickens, then remove from the heat. Divide the fillets among individual plates, spoon the sauce over and serve.
