Ingredients for akki roti

* 1 cup rice flour (akki hittu)  
* 1 small onion chopped finely  
* 3 tbsp coconut fresh grated (optional)  
* ½ carrot grated (can substitute with bottle gourd, optional)  
* 2 green chilli deseeded  
* 3 tbsp coriander leaves chopped or curry leaves  
* ½ tsp cumin or jeera  
* Salt as needed  
* ½ tsp ginger grated  
* water as needed  
* oil as needed

optional

* 3 tbsp sabsige soppu (dil leaves)  
* 1 tbsp chana dal soaked

Preparation for akki roti

1. Wash and chop onions, chilies and coriander leaves finely. Grate the carrots & ginger.

2. Add 1 cup flour to a large bowl along with salt, cumin, chopped onions, chilies, coriander leaves and carrots. Mix everything well. If using coconut & dill leaves add them too.

3. Pour water little by little and make a soft dough. The dough must be smooth and moist.

4. Divide the dough to 4 equal balls. Roll to smooth. Keep the bowl covered to prevent drying up.

5. You can make akki roti on a greased banana leaf, moist handkerchief, foil or parchment paper. 

How to make akki roti \- method 1

1. Place a ball on the moist cloth or foil or greased banana leaf. 

2. Begin to spread it lightly with your fingers to make a round roti. If the edges of akki roti crack, then dip your fingers in water and spread it.  

3. It can be made thick or thin to suit your taste. If making thick, make finger sized wholes with one and half inches apart.

Toasting akki roti

1. Heat the greased tawa on a medium high flame.  Lift the banana leaf or parchment paper and reverse it on the hot tawa. Gently peel off the leaf. 

2. When the base firms up, flip it. Toast it on both the sides. After flipping it sprinkle some oil to prevent dryness.

3. When brown spots appear, akki roti is done. Do not over cook it may turn hard.

4. While you toast the akki roti, make another one on the leaf or cloth. Transfer to the tawa after the first one is done
