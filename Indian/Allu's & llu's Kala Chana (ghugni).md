# Allu's & llu's Kala Chana (ghugni)

- See Method section

# Allu's Kala Chana (ghugni)

Lots of heated mustard oil. 

Add

Tej patta

Sabut garam masala

Sliced onions lots (as much as Chana) 1.25 cups

Ginger

Garlic

Mirchi powder

Kashmiri mirchi powder

Haldi

Dhania 1

Jeera 2

Tomato 1 chopped

Salt

Pepper

Add as much pre-soaked Chana as onion 1.5 cups

Water 1.5 cups

Pressure cook

Open and fry on medium high flame, until liquid evaporates
