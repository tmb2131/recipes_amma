My sister-in-law brought a fabulous version over from the Punjab: cumin/green chillis and the cauliflower cut in small florets fried in ghee, potatoes and minced/chopped ginger added and the whole thing then slow cooked to perfection and seasoned, finished with lemon. Maybe a pinch of ground cumin if needed, but the flavour of the cauliflower was divine. Never tasted a version that uses tomatoes which came close to this. Mind you the ghee is critical.
