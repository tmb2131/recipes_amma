Potatoes  
Oil  
Mustard seeds  
Haldi  
Cayenne  
Asafoetida   
Salt  
Curry leaves  
Boil potatoes until done (NOT mushy).   
Allow them to cook and break up by hand.   
Heat oil in pan. (Roughly 1 tbsp for 1 cup of broken potatoes).   
All mustard seeds and allow to pop.   
Lower heat and add haldi, cayenne and asafoetida, followed by potatoes.   
Salt.   
Stir and allow to cook on low heat. Stir as they form a crust on the bottom.   
Garnish with curry leaves.
