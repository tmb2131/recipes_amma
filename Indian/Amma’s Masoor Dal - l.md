# Amma’s Masoor Dal - l

- Masoor dal — 0.5 cup
- Haldi (turmeric) — 0.5 tsp
- Oil
- Ghee
- Jeera — 0.5 tsp
- Dry Red Chilli — 1 or 2
- Green (fresh) chilli — 1 or 2, slit
- Onion — 0.5, sliced thin
- Salt
- Fresh grated coconut — 1 tsp
- Lemon juice — to taste

Add 0.5 cups of red lentils, 1.25 cups of water, turmeric and a chopped tomato to crock pot. Pressure cook for 1 minute

Heat oil. Add jeera, dry chilli and onions and fry until the onion colours a bit. Add the dal and salt and bring to a vigorous boil.
Add whole, slit,fresh chillies for flavour.  Then the coconut, stir well and remove from heat.
Lemon juice and ghee.
Enjoy!!!

Preparation for masoor dal

1. Add dal to a pressure cooker (wash it a few times until the water runs clear)
2. Add chopped tomatoes, water and green chili
3. Pressure cook for 1 whistle on medium heat
4. Once the pressure goes down, open the cooker
5. Mash the dal to your liking

Tempering masoor dal

6. Add 1 tbsp of ghee in a small pan
7. Add mustard seeds, cumin, dried red chilies and garlic
8. Saute until the garlic turns slightly golden
9. Pour into the dal
10. Add salt
11. Mix and simmer for 2 minutes
12. Serve with roti or rice
