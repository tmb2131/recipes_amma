# Amma’s Yogurt

- 100% gallon of Skim milk
- 25% gallon of 1% milk (2 cups)

Boil in metal saucepan

Put saucepan in sink with water (can add ice)

Cool to 100 degrees (\\\~ 15 minutes)

Pour milk into 2 glass yogurt containers

Add slightly less than 1 scoop of starter yogurt (Erivan) to each container

Put in warming drawer on metal plates and set for 4 hours at 110 degrees
