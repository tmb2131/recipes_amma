Serves 4  
Parboiled Rice (Puzhungal Arisi)  2 cups  
 Urad dal 1 cup  
 Green chilli paste from 4 green chillies  
 Mustard seeds 1 tea spoon  
 Curry leaves few  
 Asafoetida powder 1/4 tea spoon  
 Shredded coconut 1 cup  
 Coconut oil 4 tea spoons  
 Water 5 cups  
Powder the rice and urad dal in a blender coarsely like semolina separately.  
 After powdering mix both together and keep aside.  
Heat a pan with oil and do the seasoning with mustard seeds, curry leaves and asafoetida.  
 Pour five cups of water and add salt.  
 Add the coconut to the water.  
 Once the water starts boiling keep the flame to low and gently put the powdered stuff to the water and keep stirring.  
When the stuff becomes thicker, close the pan with a lid and let it get cooked in very low flame for 10 minutes.  
 Switch off the stove.  
 Once the stuff becomes less hot, make small balls out of it.  
 Grease an idli plate, keep the balls on it and steam them out for 10 minutes.  
 Serve with any pickle of your choice or with sauce to kids.
