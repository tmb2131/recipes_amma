Beans \-1 lb, chopped small  
Turmeric \- 0.25 tsp  
Hing \- very small pinch  
Salt \- to taste  
Grated coconut \- 2 tbsp  
Curry leaves  
Sesame oil \- 1 tbsp  
Mustard seeds \- 0.5 tsp  
Urad dal \- 1.5 tsp  
Dry Red chili \- 1  
Heat oil in a wok.  Add the mustard seeds, urad dal, chili and hing. Follow with beans along with the turmeric and salt; stir well and cover.  Allow to cook on low heat until the beans are cooked but are still a little crisp.  
Turn off the heat and add the coconut and curry leaves.  
Mix well.
