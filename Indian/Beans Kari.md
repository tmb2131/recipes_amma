# Beans Kari

- Beans 1 lb, chopped small
- Sesame or coconut oil
- 1.5 tbsp
- Mustard seeds 0.75 tsp
- Red chillies 2 broken in half
- Salt to taste
- Grated coconut 0.25 cup
- Kari leaves  a few

Heat oil and add mustard seeds. When they sputter add the chillies and stir. Not add the beans and mix well. Salt.
Sprinkle some water and cover partially and allow to cook on a medium low flame taking care to stir gently every now and then. You may need to add water depending on hope tender the beans are.
Allow the water to evaporate fully at the end.
Turn off the flame, add grated coconut and curry leaves.
