# Beans potato sabzi

- Beans 500 gms, cut into 1” pieces
- Potatoes 200 gms, cut as above
- Oil 1.5 tbsp
- Mustard seeds 0.50 tsp
- Jeera seeds 0.25 tsp
- Haldi
- Cayenne
- Asafoetida, small pinch
- Dhania jeera powder

Heat oil. Add mustard seeds and jeera and asafoetida.
Add potatoes and allow to colour on medium heat. Add beans. Stir well.
Sprinkle salt and a tablespoon of water.  Allow to cook on medium low. Add spices.
Cook until done.
The vegetables should be soft and dry.
