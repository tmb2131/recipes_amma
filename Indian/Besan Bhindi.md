# Besan Bhindi

- Small Tender Okra/Bhindi — 20 — 25
- Oil — 2 tsp
- Asafoetida/Hing — A Pinch
- Cumin Seeds — 1 tsp
- Water — 2 tbsp
- For Masala/Stuffing
- Besan — 3 tbsp
- Roasted Crushed Peanuts — 3 tbsp [optional]
- Cayenne — 1 tsp
- Haldi — 1/2 tsp
- Coriander Powder — 1 tsp
- Amchur — 1 tsp
- Roasted jeera — 1 tsp
- Salt — To Taste
- Oil — 2 tsp

<br />

1. In a large bowl mix all the ingredients listed under 'For Masala', adjust salt and spice if required.
2. Wash, pat dry and cut both ends of okra, cut into 1 inch pieces.
3. Heat a oil in a pan or wok add hing, cumin seeds and allow to crack.  Add okra and saute until golden brown for 3-4 minutes.
4. Now add chickpeas mixture and stir well.  Keep on slow flame cover and cook for 14-15 minutes or until okra gets fully cooked but still crunchy. Keep stirring in intervals.
5. Sprinkle 1-2 tbsp of water mix again cover and cook again for 4-5 minutes.
