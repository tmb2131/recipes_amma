Prep 5 min  
Cook 15 min  
Makes 4  
120g kesar mango pulp (fresh or from a tin)  
200g full-fat Greek yoghurt  
160g double cream  
160g sweetened evaporated milk  
Heat the oven to 160C (150C fan)/335F/gas 3½. If you’re using fresh mango, scoop the flesh out into a bowl and use the back of a spoon to mash it to a smooth pulp (you can use a food processor to do this, too).  
Stir in the yoghurt, cream and milk, then decant into four ramekins.  
Bake for 15 minutes, then remove from the oven and leave to cool to room temperature.  
Serve with extra pieces of fresh mango or a drizzle of the tinned pulp on top.
