# Broad beans curry

- For 2
- Beans 0.5 lbs or so
- Potato 1 medium
- Oil 1 tbsp
- Kaala jeera (onion seeds) 0.33 tsp
- Dry red chilli 1
- Turmeric 0.33 tsp
- Salt to taste
- Nei to garnish

Top and tail beans and cut into small pieces.
Pare one medium potato and cut into slender pieces (keep cut potatoes soaked in water).
Heat oil.
While oil heats, drain water from potatoes and dry them with a paper towel.
Add kaala jeera and chilli to hot oil, stir once and then add the potatoes, a pinch of salt, stir and cover.
After a minute or so add the beans and the turmeric, stir.
Sprinkle some water, salt, cover and allow to cook on medium low heat.
Check for doneness after 5 minutes and more frequently thereafter.
When done, remove, add nei and serve.
