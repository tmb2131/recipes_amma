Cabbage 1 small, shredded  
Tomato 1, chopped  
Potato 1 small, boiled (Optional)  
Oil  
Jeera 0.5 tsp  
Bay leaf 1  
Dhania Powder 1tsp  
Turmeric 0.5 tsp  
Chilli powder to taste  
Ginger \- 0.5 tsp, grated  
Salt to taste

Heat oil. Add jeera and bay leaf then the cabbage. Stir well. Add spices, ginger and salt. Mix well. Add tomato stir, cover and leave on medium low heat. Check every few minutes for doneness and sprinkle water if necessary. Add potato if using shortly before removing from heat.
