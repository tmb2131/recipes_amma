# Cauliflower - Gobi sabzi

- Cauliflower - 1 medium, cut into 1.5 inch pieces
- Oil - 3 tablespoons
- Butter - I teaspoon
- Cumin - 1 teaspoon
- Turmeric -0.5 teaspoon
- Chilli powder - to taste
- Corriander powder - I tablespoon
- Ginger grated - 2 teaspoons
- Tomato - I medium, chopped
- Salt - to taste

> Heat oil and add cumin.\
> Add cauliflower and fry at high heat until some spots appear.\
> Add turmeric, chilli powder, coriander powder, ginger and mix well.\
> Add salt, mix and then tomatoes.\
> Cover for a bit, leave on low heat.  Stir every now and then till cooked.\
> Add butter, stir and allow moisture to evaporate.
