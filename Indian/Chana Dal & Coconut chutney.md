Grated coconut \- 0.5 cup  
Toasted chana dal \- 0.5 cup  
Salt \- to taste  
Hing \- a pinch  
Tamarind \- large marble sized ball  
Red chillies \- to taste   
Green chillies \- to taste  
Oil \- 1 tsp   
Seasoning  
Mustard seeds  
Onion \- 0.5 small, thinly sliced  
Curry leaves  
Fry red chillies in a little oil and grind all the ingredients (except the seasoning).  
Heat remaining oil, allow mustard seeds to support and fry onions until browned.  
Add to season and season.

* Now grind them to chutney adding some timewater. After grinding add some more water and bring them to milk shake consistency and keep aside.  
* Heat a pan with 2 tea spoons of oil.  
* Add mustard seeds and after they splutter add 2 tb spoons of thinly sliced onions and some curry leaves.  
* Fry till the onions become translucent. Switch off the stove and wait till it becomes cold.  
* Add to the chutney and mix well.
