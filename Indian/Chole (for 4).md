# Chole (for 4)

- Garbanzo beans 2 cans
- Oil 2 tablespoons
- Onions 1 large, chopped small
- Garlic 4 cloves, diced very fine
- Ginger 2 inch piece, grated or diced very fine
- Bay leaf 1
- Whole Cumin 1 teaspoon
- Tomatoes 2 medium, chopped small
- Turmeric 1 teaspoon
- Cayenne 1/4 teaspoon (for very mild, else to taste)
- Corriander powder 1 tablespoon
- Cumin powder 2 teaspoons

Drain Chickpeas

In a large saucepan, add oil and when hot add cumin seed and when they sizzle add bay leaf
Add onions and fry well with a little salt and when they begin to soften and then brown add garlic and ginger and fry well.

Add the spice powders stir well and then the tomatoes and salt.  Now keep frying until the tomatoes move as one mass glistening with oil.

Now add chick peas stir until well coated.  Add water to desired consistency.
