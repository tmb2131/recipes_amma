**Ingredients**

**For the salad**

* 1 [tomato](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/tomato), finely chopped  
* 4 tbsp finely chopped [cucumber](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/cucumber)  
* 1 green [chilli](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/chilli), finely chopped  
* 2 tbsp finely chopped [onion](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/onion)  
* handful [coriander](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/coriander) leaves and stalks, chopped  
* 2 tbsp salted [peanuts](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/peanut), roughly chopped  
* 1 heaped tbsp desiccated [coconut](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/coconut)  
* ¼ tsp grated [ginger](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/ginger)  
* [salt](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/salt), to taste

**For the spice mixture**

* 1 tsp [vegetable oil](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/vegetable_oil)  
* ¼ tsp black [mustard seeds](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/mustard_seeds)  
* few [curry leaves](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/curry_leaves)  
* 1 tbsp [lemon juice](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/lemon_juice), or to taste

**Method**

1. To make the salad, mix together all the ingredients in a bowl.  
2. To make the spice mixture, heat the oil in a small pan and add the mustard seeds. Once they begin to splutter, add the curry leaves and remove from the heat. Add the lemon juice and stir.  
3. To serve, pour the spice mixture over the salad and stir to coat. Taste and adjust the seasoning if necessary.

Cucumber & Roasted Peanut salad  
1 cup Roasted Peanuts (Moongphali)  
2 Tomatoes , finely chopped  
1 Cucumber , peeled and finely chopped  
1 Onion , finely chopped  
1 Green Chilli , finely chopped  
Salt , to taste  
1 teaspoon Chaat Masala Powder  
1 tablespoon Lemon juice

1. To begin making the Peanut Cucumber Salad Recipe, in a mixing bowl, combine the roasted peanuts, along with the finely chopped tomatoes, cucumber, onion, and green chilli.  
2. Now add salt to taste, chaat masala and lemon juice and toss Peanut Cucumber Salad well.
