# Dal palak

- 1/2 cup Yellow Moong Dal or Red Dal
- 250 gms frozen chopped spinach(Palak) or equivalent, fresh
- 1 onion chopped
- 1 tomato chopped
- 1 inch knob ginger, roughly chopped
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 small piece cinnamon
- 0.5 tsp fennel seeds
- 1 whole red chilli (or more to taste) or chili powder
- 0.5 tsp turmeric (Haldi)
- Salt , to taste
- 1 tbsp oil (olive or plain, NOT SESAME)
- Ingredients for seasoning
- 1 tablespoon Ghee
- 1/2 teaspoon Cumin seeds (Jeera)
- 2 Dry red chillies

Cook dal with turmeric in Instant pot.

When done add the spinach and a pinch of salt and allow to simmer for a few minutes.

Grind tomato, ginger , garlic cinnamon fennel and chilli.

Heat oil and add onions. Fry for 5 minutes until it colours.

Add ground paste and stir on medium heat.  Add some salt.  You may need another tsp or two of oil.  Fry until the mixture moves as a mass.  It should start exuding some of the oil.

Add the cooked dal and spinach to the mixture.  Mix well.  Simmer for a minute and remove from heat.

Add 2 tsps of ghee

Serve with rice and raita.
