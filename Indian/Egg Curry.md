# Egg Curry

- Eggs, hard boiled — 8
- Onion, chopped — 1
- Tomato, chopped — 1
- Dhania — 1.5 ***tbsp
- Saunf — 1.5 TEASPOON
- Jeera — 0.75 TEASPOON
- Haldi — 0.75 tsp
- Salt — to taste
- Chilli powder — to taste
- Green chillis, whole — 2
- Vegetable oil — 2 tbsp
- Tej patta
- Whole jeera
- Coriander leaves
- Lemon

Grind onion, tomato, dhania, jeera & saunf.
Heat oil. Add jeera and tej patta, followed by ground mixture, haldi, chilli powder & salt.
Sit on medium low heat until well done and the mixture glistens with oil.
Add boiled eggs and mix gently. Add water and bring to a simmer.
Turn off heat and garnish with coriander leaves and lemon.
