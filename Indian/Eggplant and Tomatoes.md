# Eggplant and Tomatoes

- 1 eggplant
- 4 potatoes
- 2 onions
- 2 garlic
- 4 small tomatillos
- 1 cucumber
- 4 very small radishes
- Lots of basil

<br />

Peel and chop potatoes. Add to hot oil and brown slightly. Then add sliced onions and some chopped garlic and fry well. You may need to add some more oil before adding chopped eggplant. Brown slightly. Then spices - turmeric, cayenne, coriander. And tomatillos. Salt. Cover and cook. Maybe basil on top?

Salad of cucumber and radish plus yoghurt.

Good luck.
