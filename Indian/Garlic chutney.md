# Garlic chutney

- Shallots sliced 1
- Dried chillies 3
- Coconut oil
- Tamarind small marble
- Grated coconut 1 cup
- Green chillies 2
- Garlic cloves 3
- Salt
- Coriander leaves 1 sprig

To prepare the garlic chutney

Heat coconut oil in a pan, 

Sauté the shallots, garlic and dried chillies.

Grind this sautéed mixture along with tamarind and salt in a mixer jar.

Now add the grated coconut, green chillies, 3 more garlic cloves and coriander leaves...
