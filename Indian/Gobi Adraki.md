# Gobi Adraki

- Cauliflower large — 1.5 inch pieces
- Oil 3 tablespoons
- Cumin seeds 1 teaspoon
- Green chillies slit 3
- Ginger grated 1 tbsp
- Turmeric powder 0.5 tsp
- Cayenne to taste
- Cumin powder 1 tsp
- Coriander 1 tsp
- Salt to taste
- Yogurt 3 tbsp
- Ginger thin strips
- Tomato 1, chopped
- Corriander leaves for garnish

(Keep 2 boiled potatoes handy.  If the dish is too spicy, break up the potatoes by hand and add before the ginger strips and tomatoes.  Mix and fry for a minute or two.)

Heat oil in a wok and add cumin seeds. When they begin to change colour,  add cauliflower and stir. Sauté on medium/low flame until they begin to brown in places.
Now add salt, green chillies and ginger paste. Sauté carefully until the ginger paste is somewhat dry.
Add turmeric, cayenne, coriander and cumin.  Follow with yogurt and mix gently until the yogurt begins to dry up. Cover and cook on medium/low heat till done. Check, and sprinkle water if need be.
Add ginger strips and tomatoes stir. Garnish with coriander leaves.
