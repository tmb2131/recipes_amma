Boil chana dal with a dash of haldi but not to a pulp. Should be slightly qgrainy   
Keep aside.    
Cut sliced coconut into small squares and fry these squares in ghee to a golden brown along with a handful of raisins   
Add a tiny bit more of ghee into the same kadai / vessel   
Season the dal with a bay leaf   
Jeera , 1 or 2 cardamoms , 1or 2 dry red chillies 🌶 and thin strips of ginger.  
Add the fried coconut and raisins into the seasoned dal   
Add some chopped green chillies into the dal mixture salt to taste . For the typical bengali taste add sugar to taste   
Add a little water  for the dal to cook and simmer in a   
Not too much as the consistency  of the cholar dal is to be thick ish not the thin variety meant for bhaat
