Basmathi rice   \- 1.5 cups   
Nei  \- 1 tbsp  
Cumin seeds  \- 1 tsp  
Salt to taste   
Water 2 cups   
Wash rice, drain and keep aside.   
Heat nei, add rice, fry until fairly dry.   
Add cumin seeds and salt and stir.   
When well mixed, add water and set up in Instant Pot.   
Remove and fluff up.
