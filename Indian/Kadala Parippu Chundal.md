# Kadala Parippu Chundal

- Kadala Parippu — 0.33 cups
- Dry chilli — 1
- Curry leaves — a few
- Grated coconut — 1tbsp
- Perungayam
- Salt
- Coconut oil & nei (together) 1 tbsp
- Mustard seeds

Soak & cook dal until it mashes between your fingers while holding its shape.  Drain and set aside.
Heat the fat, add mustard seeds & chilli(whole).  And Perungayam.
Lower heat.
Tip in the dal & salt and mix until warmed through.
Remove from heat.
Garnish with coconut and curry leaves.
