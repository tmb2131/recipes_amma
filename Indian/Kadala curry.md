# Kadala curry

- Kala chana 0.5 cup
- Haldi
- Shallot, medium, roughly chopped, 1
- Dhania 1.5 tbsp
- Coconut 0.5 cup
- Black pepper 5
- Red chili to taste
- Coconut oil 1 tbsp
- Mustard seeds 0.5 tsp
- Red chili 1
- Curry Patta,  a few

Soak chana, cook until soft and set aside.
Heat 1 tsp oil and add dhania, black pepper and red chili. Fry for a bit and add shallot. When the dhania has started to brown an the shallots have softened, add half the coconut and allow it to change color.  Remove and cool, then grind to a rough paste with the remaining coconut.
Set up the cooked chana along with some cooking liquid and bring to a simmer.  Mix the ground paste and salt and bring to a low simmer for a few minutes and remove from heat.
Heat the remaining oil, add the mustard seeds, chili and curry leaves. Pour over kadala. Serve.
