**You Need:**

* Spinach – 2 bunches  
* Dry red chilies – 1  
* Garlic cloves – 2, sliced  
* Asafoetida \- 2 pinches  
* Mustard oil & vegetable oil mixed– 1 tbsp  
* Salt – small pinch

Heat the oils to smoking point. Reduce heat, add red chili, let it darken, then add garlic. Stir. When the garlic colours, add the asafoetida.  
Add the spinach and salt and mix well.  
The spinach will begin to give off water in a minute. Add a tablespoon of more water, cover and cook.  
Remove when is done.
