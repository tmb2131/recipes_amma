# Katthirikkai curry

- Baingan, cut into larger than 1 — inch square pieces — 4 cups
- Mustard seeds — 0.5 tsp
- Jeera — 0.5 tsp
- Hing — a pinch
- Haldi — 0.5 tsp
- Chilli powder — 0.25 tsp (for spicy version only)
- Rasam powder — 0.75 tsp or more to taste
- Oil — more generous than usual
- Salt
- Corriander leaves

Use iron skillet.
Heat oil and pop mustard seeds and jeera. Add hing, then baingan and
salt and stir briskly.  Sprinkle a little water, cover and allow to cook on fairly high heat for a few minutes, stirring now and then to keep the vegetable from burning.
When the baingan has wilted and browned somewhat, add the spices and mix well.  Fry for a while and take off the heat. Garnish with corriander leaves.
