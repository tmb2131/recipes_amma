# Lemon Rasam

- Boiled Tuvar Dal — 1/3 cup
- Tomato — 1 large, chopped
- Rasam powder — 0.5 tsp
- Green chillies — 2
- Ginger — 1.5 inch piece
- Turmeric
- Hing to taste
- Salt
- Coriander leaves
- Curry leaves
- Lemon juice — 2+ tbsp
- Ghee
- Mustard seeds
- Dry Red chilli 1
- Hing

Grind the green chillies with the ginger and keep aside.
Take a saucepan with 1.5 cups of water.  To this add the turmeric powder,,rasam powder, hing, salt, the ground paste and tomatoes.

Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes.

Mash the tuvar dal well in 3/4
cup of water and add to the rasam.

Allow to simmer for two minutes.

Remove from heat and add the lemon juice. Check and adjust for taste.

Heat the ghee is a small pan and add the mustard seeds and chilli, a pinch of hing and a few of the curry leaves. When ready pour over rasam and add the remaining curry and coriander leaves.
