# Mango Chutney

- Large, unripe mango — 1, peeled and cut into thin, irregular slices (I used the hardest mango in the supermarket)
- Green chilli — 1
- Haldi — 0.25 tsp
- Salt — to taste
- Jaggery — half the weight of the prepared mango
- Chilli powder — 0.25 tsp
- Jeera powder — 0.5 tsp
- Oil — 1 tsp
- Neem flowers
- Water

Put the mango slices with a quarter cup of water in a heavy bottomed saucepan, along with the green chilli, haldi and a pinch of salt.
Allow it to cook until soft adding more water if necessary.  Mash the chilli in with the mango.  Add the jaggery to the mix and allow it to simmer until it becomes jammy.  Add the chilli and jeera powders and mix well.  Check flavours.
Heat oil until hot and turn off the stove. Add the neem flowers and pour the mixture over the chutney.
