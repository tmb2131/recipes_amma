Masoor dal \- 1 cup  
Haldi \- 0.5 tsp  
Oil  
Ghee  
Panch phoran 0.75 tsp  
Dry Red Chilli \- 1 or 2  
Green (fresh) chilli \- 1 or 2, slit  
Onion \- 0.5, sliced thin  
Salt  
Fresh grated coconut \- 1 tsp (optional)  
Lemon juice \- to taste; dal should be tart.  
Lemon zest  
Boil masoor with haldi and the spiced onions.  
Heat oil. Add panch phoran and dry chilli. Add the dal and salt and bring to a vigorous boil.   
Add whole, slit,fresh chillies for flavour.  Then the coconut, stir well and remove from heat.   
Lemon juice and ghee.   
Also zest. You can also add  a slice or two of the zested portion of the lime (not to be eaten).   
Enjoy\!\!\!
