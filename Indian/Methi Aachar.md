1/4 cup [**fenugreek (methi) seeds**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-fenugreek-seeds-991i) , soaked for 2 hours and drained  
2 tbsp [**oil**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-oil-671i)  
1/2 tsp [**cumin seeds (jeera)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cumin-seeds-381i)  
1/4 tsp [**asafoetida (hing)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-asafoetida-113i)  
2 [**whole dry kashmiri red chillies**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-whole-dry-kashmiri-red-chillies-332i)  
2 [**cloves (laung / lavang)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cloves-322i)  
1 small stick [**cinnamon (dalchini)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cinnamon-346i)  
1/2 cup [**chopped jaggery (gur)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-chopped-jaggery-946i)  
1/2 cup [**sugar**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-sugar-278i)  
1 tsp [**chilli powder**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-chilli-powder-339i)  
1 tbsp [**coriander-cumin seeds (dhania-jeera) powder**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-coriander-cumin-seeds-powder-375i)  
1 tbsp [**raisins (kismis)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-raisins-597i)  
1 tsp [**dried mango powder (amchur)**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-dried-mango-powder-148i)  
[**salt**](http://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-salt-418i) to taste  
Heat the oil in a pressure cooker, add the cumin seeds, asafoetida, red chillies, cloves and cinnamon and sauté on a medium flame for a few seconds.  
Add the soaked and drained fenugreek seeds, 1 cup of water, jaggery, sugar, chilli powder, coriander-cumin seeds powder, raisins, dried mango powder, and salt, mix well and pressure cook for 1 whistle.  
Switch on the flame and cook on a medium flame for 5 to 7 minutes, while stirring occasionally.  
Remove from the flame and allow it to cool completely.
