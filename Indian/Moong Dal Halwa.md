# Moong Dal Halwa

- Moong Dal — 100 gm
- Nei — 100 gm
- Mawa — 50 gm
- Sugar — 150 gm
- Cardamom powder — 1/8th tsp
- Almond flakes to garnish

Wash the moong dal and soak it in water for 4 to 5 hours.
Drain and grind somewhat coarsely without too much water.

On a medium flame, heat the frying pan, add ghee and moong dal and fry , stirring all the time. The moong dal should not stick to the frying pan and the nei should start to separate from the dal.

In a different pan, cook the mawa on low a flame until it changes colour slightly.  Mix it into the moong dal.
Heat sugar and and water ( volume 1:1) in a saucepan.  After the sugar has dissolved completely allow to simmer for a few more minutes on a low flame.

Add the syrup to the moong dal.  Stir the mixture on a low flame. When the mass starts to glisten and the nei begins to separate in a few minutes, the halwa is done.

Remove from stove and mix the the cardomom.
Garnish with almond flakes. Serve warm.
