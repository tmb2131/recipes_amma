# Mung dal halva

- Mung dal soaked & ground coarse 1 cup
- Ghee 1 cup
- Besan 1 Tbsp
- Milk 1.5 cups
- Sugar 1 cup
- Mava 0.75 cups
- Saffron
- Elaichi powder

***

**Step 1** Heat ghee in A casserole. Add besan and mix well. Add moong dal, lower heat and mix well.

**Step 2**
Sauté, stirring continuously, till golden brown or for 20-25 minutes.

**Step 3**
Heat ½ cup milk in a aan. Add generous pinch of saffron and mix well for 1 minute. Switch off heat.

**Step 4**
Heat sugar and 1 cup water in another pan. Cook till sugar dissolves completely.

**Step 5** Add saffron milk to moong dal and mix well.

**Step 6** Add remaining milk and keep stirring.Add khoya in saffron milk pan, mix and heat for 2 minutes.

**Step 7**
Add khoya to moong dal mixture alongwith cardamom powder, some almond slivers and sugar syrup.

**Step 8**
Mix well, cover and cook on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring once a while.Remove lid, mix once. Switch off heat and transfer in a serving dish.
