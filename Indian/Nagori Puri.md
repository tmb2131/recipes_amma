Ingredients Of Nagori Puri

* 1 Cup maida (refined flour)  
* 2 tbsp ghee  
* 1/4 tsp ajwain  
* to taste salt  
* 2 tbsp yogurt  
* Oil or ghee (for frying)

How to Make Nagori Puri

* 1\.  
   Mix the maida, ghee, ajwain, salt and yogurt and knead to a stiff dough.  
* 2\.  
   Roll out small rounds and fry first over high heat and then over low till brown and cooked through.  
* 3\.  
   Serve hot with a rasedaar aloo of your choice.
