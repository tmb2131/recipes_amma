Serves 4   
2 tablespoons oil  
1/2 medium onion, halved and thinly sliced  
1/2 tsp grated ginger  
2 (1-inch) cinnamon sticks  
1 cup masoor dal (orange lentils), picked over and rinsed  
Water  
Salt  
1 lemon, add to taste  
1/2 small onion, chopped  
2 small clove garlic, thinly sliced   
2 dry red chillies, whole  
1/2 serrano or jalapeno chili, chopped, with seeds  
2 bay leaves  
1 tablespoon chopped cilantro  
Ghee  
Heat 1.5 tablespoons of oil in a saucepan over medium-low heat. Add red chillies, cinnamon, sliced onion and ginger.  Cook, stirring, until tender. Add dal. Cook, stirring often, 5 minutes. Add 2.5 cups water. Bring to boil.  Transfer to a pot and cook in instant pot on High Pressure for 20 minutes.  
Heat remaining oil in small pan. Add chilli, bay leaves and sliced onions. Cook, stirring, until onion is browned. Then add sliced garlic and allow to cook but not brown.  Add mixture to lentils along with salt and stir. Remove bay leaves. Add lemon juice to taste. Turn into serving bowl and sprinkle with cilantroand some ghee. Serve hot.
