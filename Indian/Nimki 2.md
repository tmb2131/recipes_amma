(13 Nimkis)  
Dough  
Plain flour 0.75 cup  
Salt   
Kala jeera   
Ghee 2 tsp  
Filling  
2 tsp flour  
2 tsp ghee  
Make a tight dough. Allow to rest for 30 minutes.  Roll into large circle, spread filling, roll into cylinder and cut into 13 pieces.  Roll each with the swirled part facing sideways. Fold into triangle, prick both sides and fry on medium/ low heat.
