# Parippu Ushili

- French Beans — 1 1/2 cup chopped
- Tur Dal — 2/3 cup
- Chana dal — 2 tbsp
- Red Chilli — 2 or to taste
- . Asafoetida — 1/4 tsp
- Salt to taste
- For the seasoning
- Oil — 1 1/2 tbsp
- Mustard seeds — 1 tsp
- Curry leaves — 1 sprig
- Asafoetida/hing

Wash and soak tur dal and bengal gram together for 1 hour. Drain the water completely and grind it coarsely with red chillies, asafoetida and salt.
String beans and chop finely. Steam with a little salt until soft Keep aside.
Spread the the coarsely ground dal on a greased plate and steam for 12 minutes or until cooked (a toothpick when inserted should come out clean).
Once cooked remove  and leave to cool for a few minutes. Then crumble the dal with your hand until there are no
large lumps.
Heat oil in a wok, add mustard seeds, when it splutters, add hing and curry leaves. Then add the crumbled dal, mix well and cook on medium flame for 5-6 minutes.
Add the cooked beans (squeezed dry) and mix well with the crumbled dal. Cook covered for another 2-3 minutes and remove from heat.
