Rice 1 cup   
Moong dal 0.75 cup,   
lightly dry roasted  
Water 5 cups  
Full fat milk 1 cup  
Perumgayam  
Turmeric  
Jeera 0.75 tsp  
Black pepper 0.75 tsp  
Ghee 3 tbsp  
Grated ginger 1 tsp  
Cashew nuts, a handful  
Green chilli seeded 1, finely chopped  
Mix rice and roasted dal, rinse, add turmeric and milk and water. Cook in Instantpot (high pressure for 25 minutes)  
Coarsely powder cumin and pepper.   
Heat ghee and add powdered mixture, then ginger, chillis and perumgayam. Pour mixture over cooked rice and dal.  
Fry cashews and top.
