# Posto Borar Jhal

- Posto 2 tbsp + 1 tsp
- Split pea 1/3 cup
- Nigella seeds 0.5 tsp
- Green Chili 1
- Regular oil 3 — 4 tbsp
- Mustard Oil to flavour
- Cumin Seeds 1.5 tsp
- Turmeric
- Cashews 10
- Cumin Powder
- Dry chillies 2
- Asafoetida pinch
- Potatoes 1 large, cubed
- Sugar large pinch
- Salt
- Dhaniya/jeera powder 1 tsp
- Ginger paste 1 tsp
- Cayenne to taste
- Tomato 1 medium
- Yoghurt 2 tbsp
- Bhaja masala 0.75 tsp
- Ghee to top

Soak spilt peas for two hours in tepid water
Blitz poppy seeds in blender and then add soaked peas.  Add salt and grind. Add turmeric &  nigella seeds & mix.  Set aside.

In a pan toast 1 tsp jeera, 1 tsp poppy seeds and cashews.   Remove and cool. Then grind.
Shallow-fry ground paste in small balls. Cook until golden brown.
Retain i tbsp oil and add 0.5 tsp jeera, 2 whole dry chillies and asafoetida.
To this add potatoes.  Stir. Add a large pinch of sugar. Mix.
Add dhaniya-jeera powder, turmeric, cayenne and ginger paste.  Stir.  Then chopped tomatoes followed by yoghurt.
Cover and simmer for ten minutes. Then add the ground poppy seed mixture.  Add water and cook potatoes.  Finally add boras and bhaja masala. Top with ghee.
