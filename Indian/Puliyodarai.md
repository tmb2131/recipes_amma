# Puliyodarai

- For Pulikachal Powder:
- Coriander seeds — 2 tbsp
- Black Pepper — 0.5 tsp
- Fenugreek seeds — ½ tsp
- Curry leaves — 2 stems
- Red chillis — 4
- ---
- Ingredients for Pulikachal:
- Tamarind - 1 large marble
- Turmeric powder - 1 Tsp
- Chana dal - 2 tbsp
- Urad dal - 2 tbsp
- Red chilies - 5 broken
- Peanuts - 2 Tbsp
- Asafetida - 0.25 tsp
- Sesame oil - ½ cup
- Jaggery - 1 tsp
- Curry leaves 1 stem
- Salt - to taste
- ---
- Raw Rice                     2  cup
- Water                          2 1/2 - 3 cup
- Turmeric Powder          ¼ tsp
- Pulikachal                    6-7 Tbsp
- Gingili Oil                     4 Tsp
- Salt                             to taste
- Seasoning: Peanuts, curry leaves, cashews (Fry it in oil and keep aside)

***

Dry roast  ingredients for Pulikachal Powder and powder coarsely. Divide into 2 parts — one part for pulikachal paste and other part for rice. Keep aside.

***

**Method for Pulikachal:**

• Soak the tamarind in hot water for ½ hour.  Make tamarind into a thick pulp.

• Heat the thick bottom pan add oil, when oil  add mustard seeds. When mustard seedstart spluttering, add dhals, chillies, curry leaves and Hing on it. Fry till the dhals, curry leaves are golden brown. Now add the tamarind pulp, salt, ½ of the ground powder and Jaggery.

• When pulp gets thicken oil will ooze out. Switch off the stove. 

• Transfer into airtight container.

***

**Method for rice:**

• Wash and clean the rice.

• Add water, oil, turmeric powder, salt,  3 tbsp of Pulikachal paste.

• Cook it in the Electric rice cooker.

• After rice is well cooked, transfer the rice into big plate. Add above seasonings, the left over part of the powder (PULIKKACHAL POWDER) , Pulikkachal paste and mix well with flat ladle, so rice will not get smashed.

• Serve with Vadams, Fried pappad.
