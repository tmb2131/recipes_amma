Chana dal – ½ cup  
Water   
Haldi – 0.25 tsp  
Oil   
Cinnamon \- 1 small piece  
Cloves \- 2  
Cardamom \- 1  
Ginger – grated  
Green chilies – 2, chopped fine  
Onion – 1, chopped  
Tomato – 1, chopped  
Chili powder – 0.25 tsp  
Coriander powder – 1 tsp  
Turmeric powder – a pinch  
Garam masala – ½ tsp  
Oil   
Mustard seeds – ½ teaspoons  
Cumin seeds – ½ teaspoon  
Dry red chili – 1  
Cook chana dal with haldi.   
Now heat the oil a pan on medium heat. Once hot add chopped chillies and then the onions.  When they brown add the ginger.  Stir well and then add the tomatoes and fry until it turns as a mass.   
Then add haldi, red chili powder and coriander powder.   
Mix it and cook it for a minute. Add the garam masala.   
Add cooked dal. Add extra water if needed. Bring to a boil.  
Heat the oil. Once hot add mustard seeds and let them pop. Then add cumin seeds and let the sizzle.  
Then add dried red chili. Pour over dal and add ghee.
