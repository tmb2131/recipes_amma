Tuvar dal \- boiled 0.5 cup  
Tamarind \- tightly packed, marble sized soaked in   
                  1 cup of warm water  
Rasam powder \- 2.0 \+tsps  
Hing \- a pinch  
Haldi \- 0.5 tsp  
Chilli powder \- pinch  
Tomato \- 1, quartered  
Salt \- to taste   
Cilantro \-  a few leaves  
Ghee \- 1 tsp  
Mustard seeds  
Hing  
Two grinds of pepper  
Extract pulp from tamarind and to the water add rasam powder, hing, haldi, chilli powder, salt and tomato.   
Bring to a boil and allow to simmer until the mixture reduces by a third.   
Take 3+ tablespoons of boiled tuvar dal and mash well in .075 cup of water and add to rasam on stove. (the remainder of the dal isto be served at the table).   
Slowly bring to a simmer again and adjust for taste.  
Heat ghee and add mustard seeds, hing and pepper and pour over rasam.  Garnish with cilantro.   
\*\*If the rasam needs more acid squeeze some lemon juice into it.
