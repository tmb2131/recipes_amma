# Rasam (3)

- 4 whole red chillies (Guntur or another hot variety)
- 3 teaspoons coriander seeds
- 1.5 teaspoons cumin seeds
- 0.5 teaspoon of fenugreek seeds
- 0.5 teaspoon of black pepper
- a sprig of curry leaves
- a dash of asafoetida

All to be roasted in a little dab of ghee till they change colour. Then, finely grind.
Boil two tomatoes in about two cups of water, with the juice from a small lemon-sized ball of tamarind, salt, a generous pinch of turmeric and asafoetida.
Once it reaches boiling point, add half a cup of well-cooked toor dal and slow boil for about 10 minutes.
Add the rasam powder, a teaspoon of jaggery, boil for another five minutes and take it off the heat.
Give a garnish with ghee, mustard seeds, more cumin, curry leaves, and some fresh coriander leaves.
