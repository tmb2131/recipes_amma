(Sugandha)  
Rasedhar alu--  
peel the potatoes and cube them.Chop one big tomato into small pieces.Take a big kadai ,add some oil.vagar little rai, jeera, ajwain, kasoori methi & hing. Add a slit green chilli & karipatta.  
Add 4 Tbs of besan, some paprika, dhaniajeera powder & haldi. Stir & add the cubed potatoes, salt and roast it a little,.Then add water to immerse the alu. Add the chopped tomatoes and a hand full of peas, and a goli size puli, pinch of  gud. Boil till the alus are well cooked.  Your Rasedhar alu is ready. Garnish with kothmil.
