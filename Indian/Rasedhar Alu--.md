Peel the potatoes and cube them.  
Chop one big tomato into small pieces.  
Take a big kadai , add some oil.  
Vagar little rai, jeera, omam, kasoori methi & hing.  
Add a slit green chilli & karipatta.   
Add 4 Tbs of besan,kashmirI chilli powder,dhaniajeera powder & haldi.   
Stir & add the cubed potatoes, salt and fry for a bit  
Then add water to immerse the alus.   
Add the chopped tomatoes and a hand full of peas, a small marble sized puli and a pinch of  gur.  
Allow to boil until the alus are well cooked.  
Ur Rasedhar alu is ready.  
Garnish with kothmil.
