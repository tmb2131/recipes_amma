# Selvi's Rasam

- Thuvar dal 1.5 tbsp
- Pepper 0.5 tsp
- Jeera 0.5 tsp
- Red chilli 1
- Garlic clove 1
- Tomato 1
- Puli 1 large marble sized
- Haldi

Grind the above.
Heat pan with oil and mustard seeds
Add paste. Fry well and add water and salt.  And boil.
