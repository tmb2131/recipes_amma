All purpose flour 2 cups  
Oil 4 tbsps  
Nei 1 tbsp  
Salt 0.5 tsp  
Ice cold water \~ 0.5+ cup   
Put the floor in a large bowl and mix gently with salt.   
Pour oil over it and rub together gently with fingertips.  Add the nei and continue.   
Now sprinkle the water on the dough and mix with fingers.  When the dough comes together and is fairly stiff, roll it into a large ball.   
Now manipulate it until the ball is smooth and pliable but still stiff.   
Cover with damp towel and allow to rest.   
Filling  
Boiled potatoes
