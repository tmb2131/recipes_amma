Sorrel leaves 1+ cup  
Garlic cloves 2  
Green chili 1 (seeded, if needed)  
Red chilli 1 (seeded, if needed)  
Mustard seeds 0.5 tsp  
Urad Dal 2 tsp  
Chana Dal 2 tsp  
Coconut 1/3 cup (grated or sliced)  
Salt  
Oil  1 tsp  
Heat oil in frying pan.  Add mustard seeds and wait for them to pop.  Add the two dals and fry until they are toasted. Now add the red chili followed by the green chili and whole garlic cloves.    
Then the sorrel leaves and salt allow leaves to wilt.  Turn off heat.  
Wait for mixture to cool and grind with coconut.
