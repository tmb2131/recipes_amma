Frozen chopped spinach \- 1 pack  
Yogurt  \- 1+ cup  
Roasted cumin seed powder \- 0.5 tsp  
Cayenne \- 0.25 tsp or to taste  
Black salt \- 0.5 tsp  
Regular salt \- a pinch  
Sugar \- 2 pinches  
Boil frozen spinach with a pinch of salt on a low flame until cooked.  You may sprinkle some water to help it along but do not overcook.  
Allow to cool completely  
Beat the yogurt until smooth with regular salt and sugar.  
Squeeze the water out of the spinach.  (Do not discard.  Drink it or add to dal).   
Mix the cooled spinach into the yogurt.  Sprinkle the spices on top and mix before serving.
