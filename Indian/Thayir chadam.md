# Thayir chadam

- Bhagala Bhat
- Raw rice: 200gms
- Milk 200ml
- Yoghurt 100ml
- Cream 2 tbsp
- Salt to taste
- Asafoetida a pinch
- Green chillis 3, chopped
- Ginger 0.5 tsp grated
- Red chillis 3
- Urad dal 1 tsp
- Curry leaves
- Coriander leaves
- New rice — soak for 20 minutes
- Rice: water 1:2.5

Add milk when rice is hot so mixture is warm and soft
Add 1.5 tbsp butter and mix.
When cool add yogurt
Salt and then cream.
Temper with mustard seeds, red chillis, Urad dal, chopped chillis,  ginger, curry leaves and coriander and asafoetida in water.
Fresh coriander leaves
