# Tomato chutney (2)

- Tomatoes 3, chopped
- Onion 1, sliced
- Red chillies 2
- Salt to taste
- Seasame oil 1 table spoon
- Mustard 1/2 teaspoon
- Urad dal 1/2teaspoon
- Asafoetida powder 1/4 teaspoon
- Curry leaves few

Heat oil. Add mustard seeds, urad Dal and chillies. When the urad dal browns, add the onions and fry until almost translucent.  Add asafoetida.
Now add chopped tomatoes and fry for a bit.  Turn off stove and cool. Blend and adjust for taste.
