0.5cup [toovar (arhar) dal](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-toovar-dal-955i)  
1 tomato  
1/4 tsp [turmeric powder (haldi)](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-turmeric-powder-645i)

1 tsp [lemon](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-lemon-428i) juice or small piece of tamarind  
0.5 inch piece grated [ginger](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-grated-ginger-943i)  
2 [slit green chillies](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-Slit-Green-Chillies-1856i)  
1/4 tsp cayenne   
1 tbsp [peanuts](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-peanuts-847i)

[salt](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-salt-418i) to taste   
0.5 tsp sugar

1 tsp [ghee](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-ghee-245i)  
1 tsp [oil](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-oil-671i)

1/4 tsp [mustard seeds](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-mustard-seeds-525i)   
1/4 tsp [cumin seeds](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cumin-seeds-381i) jeera  
1/4 tsp [fenugreek seeds](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-fenugreek-seeds-991i) \- methi  
6 to 7 [curry leaves](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-curry-leaves-388i)   
2 [cloves](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cloves-322i)  
Small piece [cinnamon](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-cinnamon-346i)   
2 small [red chillies](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-round-red-chillies-1096i)   
1/4 tsp [asafoetida (hing)](https://www.tarladalal.com/glossary-asafoetida-113i)

Cilantro for garnish.

Boil dal with turmeric and tomato.

When cooked, blend together.

Add tamarind, cayenne, salt, sugar, green chillies, ginger and peanuts to dal in a saucepan and bring to a boil.

In a small pan heat oil and ghee.  Add mustard seeds, jeera, methi, red chillies, cinnamon cloves and hing.

Pour into dal and simmer for a few minutes. Garnish with cilantro and a little ghee.
