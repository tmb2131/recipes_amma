# Tuvar Dal

- 0.5cup toovar (arhar) dal
- 1 tomato
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder (haldi)
- 1 tsp lemon juice or small piece of tamarind
- 0.5 inch piece grated ginger
- 2 slit green chillies
- 1/4 tsp cayenne
- 1 tbsp peanuts
- salt to taste
- 0.5 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp ghee
- 1 tsp oil
- 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/4 tsp cumin seeds jeera
- 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds — methi
- 6 to 7 curry leaves
- 2 cloves
- Small piece cinnamon
- 2 small red chillies
- 1/4 tsp asafoetida (hing)
- Cilantro for garnish.

Boil dal with turmeric and tomato.

When cooked, blend together.

Add tamarind, cayenne, salt, sugar, green chillies, ginger and peanuts to dal in a saucepan and bring to a boil.

In a small pan heat oil and ghee.  Add mustard seeds, jeera, methi, red chillies, cinnamon cloves and hing.

Pour into dal and simmer for a few minutes. Garnish with cilantro and a little ghee.
