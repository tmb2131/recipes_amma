Regular Dosa batter fermented one 1 large bowl  
Very finely and thinly chopped onions, and green coriander leaves 1 cup each  
Finely chopped seeded tomatoes  
Pepper powder & a pinch of salt ***OR***   gunpowder   
Oil for making Dosa  
Place the dosa tawa on the stove.  
Grease it with little oil.  
Sprinkle few drops of water to see whether the tawa is hot enough. If you get good sound it is hot enough.  
Pour 2 ladles of batter on the tawa and spread it gently around the tawa.  
The Dosa need not be thin.If you are using tomato puree pour 2 tea spoons of puree on the dosa and spread gently (or) sprinkle the onions, tomatoes and coriander leaves on the Dosa.  
Also sprinkle some pepper powder.  
With the Dosa making spatula gently press the toppings to make them to settle down well on the batter.  
Drizzle some oil on and around the dosa.  
Wait for 1 minute.  
Gently flip off the dosa and give a gentle press and again drizzle some oil.  
Let the veggies get well roasted for 2 minutes.  
Now you transfer this on a plate.
