Zucchini \- 1  
Tamarind \- seedless & small marble           
                  sized  
Oil \- 1 tsp  
Dry Red Chilli \- 1  
Urad dal \- 1 tsp  
Hing \- a pinch  
Grated coconut \- 1 tbsp  
Salt \- to taste  
Garlic clove \- 1 (optional)  
Cut zucchini into smallish pieces on the bias. Heat oil in a large pan. Add chilli and urad dal. When the mixture turns brown add the tamarind & hing and turn a few times. Now add the zucchini (and whole garlic clove, if using) and fry on medium high heat until the zucchini has some brown specks. Add salt and keep on heat for less than a minute. If the zucchini lets out a lot of water, turn the heat up and stir well until nearly all the water has evaporated. (The zucchini should be partially raw.) Take the pan off the heat put the frozen coconut on top (do not stir) and allow the mixture to cool to room temperature.   
Grind in the larger container in the mixie (short pulses and a few long ones).   
Enjoy with rice and dal
