Zucchini 3 small (cut on the bias into 1-inch+ pieces)

1 small green chilli, minced  
Oil 1 tablespoon  
Mustard seeds 0.25 tsp+  
Methi seeds  0.25 tsp

Cardamom 2  
Cloves 3  
Cinnamon 0.5" stick  
(Smash these three lightly with pestle)

Haldi 0.33 tsp  
Chilli powder 0.125 tsp (1/8 tsp)  
Coriander powder 0.75 tsp  
Salt

1.5 tbsp cream \- optional

Ghee & cilantro for garnish

Heat oil on medium flame.   
Add mustard seeds and methi.   
When they sputter, the cardamom & co. can go in, followed by minced green chilli. 

Add zucchini, haldi and chilli powder. Stir to combine. Now add the coriander powder, mix and then salt.   Cover briefly and cook on medium low flame.  

After a few minutes uncover, lower flame and check for done-ness. (Optional- Finally add cream and allow to heat through. 

Pour into serving dish and top with minced cilantro.
