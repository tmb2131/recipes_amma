# Cheese mini quiches

- Makes 12
- 12 slice white bread (crusts removed)
- 20 g Salted Butter (softened)
- 1/2 cup cheddar or gruyère cheese (grated)
- 60 g minced and sauteed mushrooms
- 2 spring onion (finely sliced)
- 6 eggs (lightly whisked)

***

1 Preheat the oven to 180°C.

&#x20; 2 Use a rolling pin to lightly roll slices of bread and flatten until thin. Butter the slices of bread on one slide. Place the slices in a muffin tin, butter side down in order to create a case. Transfer into the oven and bake for 8 minutes. Remove the tin from the oven and reduce the temperature to 160°C.

&#x20; 3 Meanwhile, place the cheese, mushrooms,spring onions and eggs in a large bowl. Season with salt and pepper and mix well to combine.

&#x20; 4 Pour the mixture carefully into the toasted cases, and return to the oven to bake for a further 15-20 minutes or until set and lightly golden. Remove from the oven and allow to stand for 5 minutes, before carefully removing from the tin.

&#x20; 5 Serve warm or at room temperature.

<br />

**Toast Cups:** Preheat over to 400 degrees. Spray a muffin tin (or mini muffin tin) with cooking spray. Using a plain or fluted biscuit cutter (2.5-3 inch size for the muffin tin and 1.5-2 inch size for minis), cut a round from each slice of bread and gently press into the muffin cups. Bake until edges are golden brown (about 10 minutes) and let they cool on a wire rack. If storing for future use, cool and then put into an air tight container.
