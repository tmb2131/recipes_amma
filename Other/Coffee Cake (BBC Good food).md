* STEP 1Preheat oven to 170’C. Line two 20cm cake tins.  
* STEP 2Whisk together the eggs, olive oil, coffee, milk and sugar. Gently mix in the flour and baking powder. Divide the mixture evenly between the cake tins. Bake for 25-30 mins.  
* STEP 3To make the filling, whisk together the butter and sugar. Add the mascarpone, coffee and vanilla extract. Whisk until completely combined. Spread half the cream in between the sponges, and half the cream on top of the cake. Arrange the walnut halves on top of the cake.
