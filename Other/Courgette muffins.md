## Ingredients  
* 1 small (125g grated weight) courgette  
* 175g self-raising flour  
* 125g cheddar, grated  
* 1 egg, beaten  
* 60ml sunflower oil  
* milk  
## Method  
* STEP 1Heat the oven to 200C/fan 180C/gas 6 and line eight holes of a muffin tin with muffin cases. Coarsely grate the courgette and use clean hands to squeeze out as much liquid as possible over the sink. Tip into a bowl with the flour and most of the cheese.   
* STEP 2Crack the egg into a jug, pour in the oil, then top up with enough milk to make 250ml. Pour into the courgette bowl and mix to a smooth batter. Divide between the cases, top with the remaining cheese and bake for 20-25 minutes until risen, firm and golden brown on top.
