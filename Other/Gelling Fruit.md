Finely cut the prepared fruits or puree them or measure the fruit juice.

Mix fruit / fruit juice with extra gelling sugar in a large saucepan. Bring to a boil while stirring and cook under constant stirring for at least 3 min. Bubbly.

Foam the fruit if necessary, immediately fill to the brim in prepared glasses. Close with screw-on lids (twist-off), turn over immediately and allow to stand on the lids for about 5 minutes.
