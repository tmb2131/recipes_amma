# One Pot Pasta

- Serves 4
- 12 oz pasta
- 12 oz, halved or quartered if large
- 1 onion, thinly sliced (about 2 cups)
- 4 cloves of garlic, thinly sliced
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 sprigs basil, plus torn leaves for garnish
- 2 tbsp EVOO, plus more for serving
- Coarse salt
- Ground black pepper
- 4 1/2 cups water

Combine pasta, tomatoes, onion, garlic, red-pepper flakes, basil, oil, 2 teaspoons salt, 1/4 teaspoon pepper, and water in a large pot (the pasta should lie flat).

Bring to a boil over high heat. Boil mixture, stirring and turning pasta occasionally until pasta is al dente and water has nearly evaporated, about 9 minutes.
Season to taste with salt and pepper, divide among 4 bowls, and garnish with basil.

Serve with olive oil and Parmesan.
