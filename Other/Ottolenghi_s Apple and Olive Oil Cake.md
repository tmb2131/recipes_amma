Ottolenghi's Apple and Olive Oil Cake  
  
[https://www.bakinglikeachef.com/apple-olive-oil-cake-yotam-ottolenghi/](https://www.bakinglikeachef.com/apple-olive-oil-cake-yotam-ottolenghi/)  
  
**Total Time:** 1 hour, 30 minutes **Yield:** 10   
  
INGREDIENTS  
SCALE  
1x  
2x  
3x  
  
**For the cake:**  
* 2.8 oz (80 g) sultana raisins  
* 1/4  cup (60 ml) water  
* 2 1/4 cups (280 g) flour  
* 1/2 teaspoon ++[ground cinnamon](https://amzn.to/36hAxvf)++  
* 1 pinch of salt  
* 1/2 teaspoon ++[baking powder](https://amzn.to/3dGmCzI)++  
* 1/2 teaspoon ++[baking soda](https://amzn.to/3moAweU)++  
* 1/2 cup (120 ml) olive oil  
* 1/2 cup + 1/2 tablespoon (160 g) ++[granulated sugar](https://amzn.to/33HX82h)++  
* 1 teaspoon ++[vanilla extract](https://amzn.to/2W53pkP)++  
* 2 whole eggs  
* 2 egg whites  
* 1 teaspoon lemon zest  
* 3 Granny Smith apples  
**For the maple icing:**  
* 3.5 oz (100 g) unsalted butter  
* 1/2 cup (100 g) ++[golden brown sugar](https://amzn.to/3o6F5uw)++   
* 1/3 cup (85 ml) ++[maple syrup](https://amzn.to/3Vmff68)++  
* 7.7 oz (220 g) cream cheese  
* If needed, please refer to ++[Baking Conversion Charts](https://www.bakinglikeachef.com/category/baking-conversion-charts/)++.

INSTRUCTIONS  
1. Preheat oven to 375 F/190 C. Butter a springform ++[round cake pan](https://amzn.to/2NlG9fO)++ 8-inch/20 cm in diameter and line the bottom and sides with ++[parchment paper](https://amzn.to/2O4Na4k)++.  
2. Place sultana raisins and water in a ++[saucepan](https://amzn.to/3WKsPkP)++ and simmer over low heat until the water is completely absorbed by dry fruit. Set aside. Peel apples with a ++[vegetable peeler](https://amzn.to/2oADhR1)++, remove the heart and seeds, and cut into 1 cm cubes.  
3. **To make the cake,** place olive oil, sugar, and ++[vanilla extract](https://amzn.to/2W53pkP)++ in the bowl of a ++[stand mixer](https://amzn.to/3uQHM7F)++ and beat. Gradually add lightly beaten whole eggs and continue to mix until the mixture becomes smooth. Then add apple cubes, sultana raisins, lemon zest grated with a ++[zester grater](https://amzn.to/32OCv2c)++ and combine with a ++[rubber spatula](https://amzn.to/3WUNl1P)++. Finally, add the sifted dry ingredients (flour, ++[ground cinnamon](https://amzn.to/36hAxvf)++, salt, ++[baking powder](https://amzn.to/3dGmCzI)++, and ++[baking soda](https://amzn.to/3moAweU)++) and mix.  
4. In a clean bowl, beat egg whites with a ++[stand mixer](https://amzn.to/3uQHM7F)++ or an ++[electric mixer](https://amzn.to/3FVfEXn)++ until soft peaks. Add it into the main preparation at two times and gently combine with the spatula. Pour the batter into a cake pan, even the surface with a spoon, and bake for 90 minutes. Check the cake's readiness with a skewer: if it comes out dry, the cake is ready. Remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan.  
5. **To make the maple icing,** place softened butter, brown sugar, and ++[maple syrup](https://amzn.to/3Vmff68)++ in a bowl and beat until the mixture becomes creamy and smooth. Add cream cheese **at room temperature** and beat until the cream becomes homogeneous.  
6. **To assemble the cake, **remove the cold cake from the pan and cut it horizontally using a serrated knife. If the cake is domed after baking, cut the top to even the surface. Place the bottom of the cake on a serving platter and spread half of the maple icing. Place the top half of the cake on the cream layer and cover it with the remaining icing. Even the surface of the cake with a ++[bent spatula](https://amzn.to/2O2vxls)++ or create waves.  
  
NOTES  
1. **Bring butter and cream cheese to room temperature** 2 hours before making the cake: it is essential!  
2. **Use a round cake pan** (8-inch/20 cm in diameter) buttered and lined with parchment and ++[precut circle cake pan liners](https://amzn.to/33Dj9NF)++. A springform form facilities the removal of the cake, but a regular cake pan works as well.  
3. **Make the cake in advance,** wrap it with ++[plastic film](https://amzn.to/2n60kTF)++ and keep in the fridge for up to one week. Slice and assemble the cake with maple icing on the day of serving.
