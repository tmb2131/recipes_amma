Chili and ginger miso  
Chili and ginger miso  
  
Makes a small jarful  
150g white miso, or chickpea miso  
1 tsp crushed chilli flakes  
A bunch of spring onions, thinly sliced, including the green parts  
A small thumb-size piece of ginger, peeled and grated  
2 garlic cloves, peeled and grated  
2 tbsp mirin  
A pinch of salt  
1 tbsp fermented gochujang paste (optional)  
1 Combine all the ingredients in a small saucepan over a gentle heat. You just want to warm this for a few minutes, to get the ginger, garlic and spring onions to lose their rawness.  
2 Leave the paste to cool then transfer to a jar. It can be stored in the fridge for up to 3 weeks or frozen.
