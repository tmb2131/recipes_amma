# Succotash

- TOTAL TIME: 30 minutes
- SERVES: 4
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 12 sprigs tarragon
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 4 cups corn kernels freshly cut off cob
- Kosher salt
- 2 small zucchini, finely diced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
- Juice of ½ lemon
- 1 tablespoon minced chives (optional)

In a small pot over medium-low heat, combine cream and 3 tarragon sprigs. Bring cream to a simmer and gently cook until slightly thickened and infused by herbs, about 3 minutes.

Set a large sauté pan over medium heat and swirl in 2 tablespoons butter. Add corn and cook until kernels heat through, about 1 minute. Season with salt. Stir in zucchini and sauté until beginning to soften, about 1 minute. Stir in tomatoes and infused cream. Season with salt and cook until zucchini is tender and tomatoes’ skins begin to split, 3-4 minutes. Season with lemon juice and salt to taste. Remove pan from heat and discard tarragon sprigs.

Remove leaves from remaining tarragon sprigs and mince. Stir minced tarragon into succotash. Garnish with minced chives, if using. Serve immediately.
