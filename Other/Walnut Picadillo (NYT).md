PREPARATION  
1. Add the walnuts to a medium bowl with the tomato sauce, tomato paste, liquid aminos, vinegar, paprika, sugar, cumin and salt. Mix until fully combined; set aside.   
2. Add the oil to a large skillet and heat over medium. Once the oil is hot and shimmering, add the onion and bell pepper. Sauté for about 5 minutes until the onion is translucent and the peppers are soft.   
3. Add the serrano chile and garlic and cook until fragrant, stirring frequently, 1 to 2 minutes.  
4. Add the potatoes to the skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until the potatoes soften slightly, 5 to 8 minutes.   
5. Add the walnut mixture and cook for 10 to 15 minutes, stirring frequently, until the mixture lightly browns.   
6. Add the beer (if using), tomatoes, olives and capers, and allow to simmer for at least 15 minutes to let the ingredients meld together, stirring frequently.   
7. Serve over rice with corn tortillas.
