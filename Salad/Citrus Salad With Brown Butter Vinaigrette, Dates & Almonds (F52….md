# Citrus Salad With Brown Butter Vinaigrette, Dates & Almonds (F52…

- 1 large navel or cara cara orange, at room temperature
- 2 small blood oranges, at room temperature
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 2 tablespoons roughly chopped almonds
- 2 teaspoons red wine vinegar
- 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 1/2 cups roughly torn escarole or frisee leaves
- 5 Medjool dates, seeded and torn in halves and quarters, at room temperature
- Flaky salt

Peel the oranges with a small paring knife: Slice off the ends to create two flat surfaces. Set on one flat surface, then slice off the skin and pith, following the spherical shape. Slice each fruit crosswise into 1/2 — inch rounds.

<br />

Heat a small pot over medium heat, add the butter and melt until the milk solids start to turn golden, about 5 minutes. Stir in the almonds and keep cooking to until the milk solids are dark amber and the overall aroma is nutty, 2 to 4 minutes. As soon as the butter has browned, turn the heat off. Carefully add the vinegar and salt (some bubbling will occur).
Arrange the salad by placing the escarole flat on a serving platter. Tuck the orange rounds and dates into the escarole like a very loose fitting mosaic. While the brown butter vinaigrette is still warm, stir with a spoon and drizzle over the salad. Sprinkle with flaky salt and serve immediately.
