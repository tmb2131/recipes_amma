# Strawberry and Mixed Green Salad

- Serves 6
- 6 cups (200 g) mix of baby greens or fresh baby spinach
- 2 cups strawberries , hulled and quartered (or other berries)
- 100 g/4 oz crumbled blue cheese (such as Roquefort) or goat cheese (such as Sainte Maure)
- ½ cup chopped pecans or other nuts/candid nuts , preferably toasted
- Dressing:
- 2 tablespoons extra — virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup or honey , optional
- ¼ teaspoon table salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

1. To make the dressing:
2. In a small bowl whisk oil, vinegar, mustard, maple syrup, salt, and pepper until well combined.
3. In a large bowl place greens, strawberries, cheese, and nuts. Pour as much dressing as you want over salad mixture and toss to combine Serve immediately.
