# Courgette & Tomato Soup (BBC)

- Serves 8
- 1 tbsp ++[butter](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/butter — glossary)++
- 2 ++[onions](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/onion — glossary)++, chopped
- 1kg ++[courgette](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/courgette — glossary)++, sliced
- 1kg ++[tomato](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/tomato — glossary)++, chopped
- 2 tbsp ++[plain flour](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/flour — glossary)++
- ½ tsp ++[turmeric](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/glossary/turmeric — glossary)++
- 2l low — sodium chicken or vegetable stock from cubes
- crusty bread, to serve (optional)

* STEP 1 Melt the butter in a large pan, add the onions and courgettes, and cook for 5 mins on a medium heat, stirring occasionally.

* STEP 2 Add the tomatoes and flour. Cook for a couple of mins, stirring around to stop the flour from becoming lumpy. Add the turmeric and stock, cover and simmer for 30 mins.

* STEP 3 Purée with a stick blender, then sieve if you want a really smooth texture. Serve hot with crusty bread, if you like, or chill, then freeze for up to 2 months.
