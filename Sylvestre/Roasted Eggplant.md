# Roasted Eggplant

## Ingredients

* Eggplant
* Salt
* Pepper
* Oregano
* Cumin
* Garlic powder
* Olive oil

## Method

* Heat oven to 420.
* Halve eggplant.
* Score crisscross pattern in the flesh.
* Add salt, pepper, oregano, cumin, and garlic powder.
* Splash olive oil on eggplant.
* Put into oven for 20 minutes until top browns. Reduce to 375 for ~40 minutes.
